The fundamental theorem of arithmetic in number theory states that every positive integer number greater than 1, \(x \in \textbf{Z} where x > 1\) is a prime number or it is comprised of the unique product of prime numbers up to the order of the factors. \[x^{2}+y^{2}=r^{2}\] circle.
