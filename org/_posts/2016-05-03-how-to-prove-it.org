*Laws of Logic*

1.) DeMorgan's law:
$\ne(P\and Q) = \ne P \or \ne Q$
$\not(P\or Q) = \notP \and \notQ$
2.) Commutative law:
$P \and Q = Q\and P$
$P\or Q = Q\or P$
