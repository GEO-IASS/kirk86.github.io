*Laws of Logic*

1.) DeMorgan's law:

$\neg(P\land Q) \eq \ne P \lor \neg Q$
$\neg(P\lor Q) \eq \neg P \land \neg Q$

2.) Commutative law:

$P \land Q = Q \land P$
$P \lor Q = Q \lor P$
